Establish a command and response structure for the MQTT communication between the temperature measurement clients and the controlling entities.
Controlling entities may be a central server, a local gateway, or any other entity that needs to receive the temperature sensing data from the clients.
More details in the controlling entities to be defined.

The temperature measurement clients shall subscribe to all controlling entities automatically after startup.
From these controlling entities, the clients shall receive commands to
- to return status information about themselves (e.g. uptime, payload version (JSON data-structure), sensor configuration, etc.)
- publish their sensing data after a specific trigger (e.g. a command to start publishing, or a time-based schedule).
